Solution Architecture

It is a detailed plan that defines the fundamental structure of a software system and how its components will interact. It takes into account data management, scalability, security and system performance.

It is one of the most important stages in software development. The right architectural choice determines the long-term success of the project and facilitates future expansion and maintenance processes.
